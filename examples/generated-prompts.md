# Example world prompts

These shortened examples illustrate the three development fixtures. The app produces longer prompts plus a structured scene plan, confidence object, evidence weights, and limitations.

## 1. Known lyrical work — “A visible sanctuary reached by learning to perceive the path”

Create a coherent explorable 3D environment carved into a pale threshold-city around a luminous inner sanctuary. Keep the sanctuary visible from most regions while the traversable path repeatedly passes behind oversized limestone measuring walls. Begin in a sheltered, human-scale antechamber; widen each connected region as the musical harmony expands. At the emotional peak, open the route into an exposed monumental basin where the walls fall away and warm mineral light reaches the path. End on an elevated terrace that makes the complete journey legible. Use chalk limestone, brushed silver, translucent mineral, and restrained amber light consistently. Preserve multiple viewpoints, spatial continuity, clear foreground, midground, and background, return paths, and one persistent landmark.

## 2. Obscure lyrical song — “A living orchard inside an indifferent machine”

Build one navigable botanical-industrial world where glass-bearing trees grow through an abandoned precision works. The foreground path moves between living wood roots and smoked-glass irrigation channels; the midground tightens into repeated dark-steel service corridors; the climax opens into a vast glasshouse-machine hall containing a silent stamping press. Let transparent fruit remain delicate but structurally important, catching amber resin light against cold electric-blue reflections. Make the organic and mechanical systems physically interdependent rather than separating them into good and evil zones. Connect every area with a walkable maintenance path and preserve the orchard, press, material palette, and architectural rules from all viewpoints.

## 3. Unknown instrumental — “Separate currents gathering into one navigable surge”

Create a vast explorable 3D tidal plain made of resonant stone, shallow reflective channels, and suspended metallic spans. Start with isolated paths separated by empty distance and a low persistent horizon landmark. Gradually let the routes converge toward a turbulent central basin whose dense overlapping bridges express competing rhythmic layers. Give the musical climax the world’s largest exposed volume and strongest vertical structures without making the terrain non-navigable. After the basin, separate the paths again into a quieter elevated field where surfaces retain visible resonance patterns from their convergence. Use connected areas, multiple viewpoints, spatial continuity, clear foreground, midground, and background, and a traversable route through all major regions. Treat the interpretation as sound-led; do not imply lyrical narrative or artist intent.

## Shared negative prompt

No text, lyrics, signage, logos, recognizable people, living-artist style imitation, trademarked fictional settings, music-video recreation, disconnected dioramas, single-view composition, impossible non-navigable geometry, excessive unrelated symbols, or assumed timed animation.
